Why We Created QuizToit?

Saad and Fatima are fellow graduate students of Bahria University, Islamabad, who built the QuizToit app as their semester project. This was their first ever project in web development and a major contributor to Fatima's newfound passion in this field. From fighting over color schemes to celebrating little achievements, this project taught us how to work and solve challenges as a team.
